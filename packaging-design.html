Very Berry - UOM, Malta

Very Berry is a packaging design project that is associated to 3 of the great pillars in my life - My mum, my grandmas and my aunt. Every goodie has a woman illustrated on the packaging. This is no way associated with the connotation that “only women can cook” or “baking is for women”, however the people in my life who taught me recipes, baking tricks and how to cook are these women. The wholesome idea about this project is that, even though you are away from these people, due to the Covid-19 situation, you can still enjoy the sweet things in life with them. You can have a cookie with your Mum, a nice toast with jam with your Grandma and a cup of tea with your Aunt.
